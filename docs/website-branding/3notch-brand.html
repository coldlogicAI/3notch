01The concept

A marked trail, not a destination.

3Notch is a substrate for passing AI context across tools, models, and people. The name and mark come from the practice of carving horizontal notches into trees to signal a way through.

Travelers carved three horizontal notches into roadside trees to mark the way forward. The marks stayed long after the traveler moved on.

A packet is a notch. An inbox is a trail. The product is what makes the trail readable to anyone who comes next — another tool, another agent, another person, or you in six months.

Origin lives here. Keep it brief everywhere else.

02Color palette

Warm dark canvas, one red mark.

A restrained system. Paper on ink for the 95%. The mark red carries every meaningful accent. No bark, moss, sky, or gold — those live on the mascot, not in the brand.

04The mascot

A warm interruption inside a precise system.

The lumberjack is the brand's personality, not its theme. He appears as a small sticker, badge, or character cutout against the clean dark canvas. He does not drive the color or typographic system.

Hero character 3D · Pixar-style render
Role
Personality, not theme. Used as accent — small sticker on a terminal, a badge in a corner, a hero character against a clean dark scene. Never drives the layout or palette.
Character
Confident, not jolly. Knowing smirk. Lean face, not chubby. Hand in pocket or axe over shoulder. Full beard, red beanie, red and black plaid flannel, suspenders, jeans, brown boots.
Scene
Standing beside a tree carrying exactly three shallow horizontal notch marks. The notches are clean parallel cuts, modest in size — trail blazing marks, not deep gashes.
Colors
Stay on the character. Bark, moss, sky, gold, and the saturated flannel all live on his body and his world. They do not enter the brand palette. This keeps the system clean and lets him pop.

06Voice & tone

Direct, technical, quietly confident.

Writes like a thoughtful developer tool — short sentences, low ceremony, no marketing puff. Earns its moments of warmth through restraint everywhere else.

The brand sounds like

  • Leave a notch. Mark your trail.
  • Package the session. Send it anywhere.
  • A packet, an inbox, a way through.
  • The substrate for agentic workflows.
  • Three horizontal marks. That's the whole idea.

The brand does not sound like

  • Revolutionize your AI workflow!
  • Empowering teams to unlock context.
  • The future of agent communication.
  • Built different, ship faster.
  • Howdy, partner — let's blaze a trail.
